# Proposal notes (organizers only)

Draft material moved off the public website. Use this when writing the 3-page NeurIPS 2026 workshop proposal and 2-page organizer appendix. See [NeurIPS 2026 Workshops Guidance](https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2026/WorkshopsGuidance).

## Tagline (≤140 characters)

ML for aging biology: virtual cells, perturbations, and intervention modeling at NeurIPS 2026.

## Extended topic list (for proposal, not public site)

- Biological foundation models (transcriptomics, imaging, proteins, epigenomics, spatial biology)
- Virtual cells and world models (dynamics, perturbations, senescence, rejuvenation)
- Perturbation response prediction (CRISPR, chemical, combinatorial)
- Generative intervention design (diffusion, flow matching, latent dynamics)
- Causal ML for biology (counterfactuals, intervention discovery, experimental design)
- Aging clocks and biomarkers
- Agentic AI for scientific discovery
- Evaluation, benchmarks, reproducibility, negative results
